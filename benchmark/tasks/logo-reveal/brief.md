# Task: Logo reveal

Canvas: 1920x1080, 30fps. Total duration: **5.0 seconds**. Background: #08090B.

Create a centered logo reveal for the fictional brand **NOVA**:

- Mark: a simple geometric lockup you compose yourself from basic shapes —
  e.g. a ring or disc in **#7C5CFF** with a contrasting inner shape. Keep it
  abstract and clean; it sits above the wordmark.
- Wordmark: **"NOVA"** — wide, bold, white, generous letter spacing.
- Tagline: **"light the way"** — small, muted gray, below the wordmark.

Motion:
1. 0.0–1.2s: the mark assembles: its parts arrive with distinct motion
   (e.g. scale-in with rotation settling to 0), overlapping in time, strong
   decelerating ease.
2. 1.0–1.8s: wordmark rises into place from slightly below while fading in;
   tagline follows ~0.2s later.
3. Hold the full lockup until 4.2s. During the hold the mark should float
   subtly (slow, continuous, small amplitude) so the frame stays alive.
4. 4.2–5.0s: the whole lockup scales up slightly and fades out.

The result should read as a polished product-launch sting: balanced
composition, calm pacing, nothing jittery.
